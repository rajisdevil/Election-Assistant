The Election Process

1

Voter Registration

Ensure you are eligible and registered to vote before the deadline. Check your local state guidelines for exact dates.

2

Researching Candidates

Review the platforms, past records, and policies of the candidates. Look into local, state, and federal races.

3

Election Day

Find your polling place, bring necessary ID, and cast your ballot. Alternatively, mail in your ballot early.

4

Results & Certification

Votes are counted, results are announced, and finally certified by election officials.
